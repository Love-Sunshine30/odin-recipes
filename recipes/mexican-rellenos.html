Mexican Lasagna

Description

Authentic chile relleno recipe handed down for generations in my family.

Ingredients

  • Chile peppers
  • White Mexican cheese
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Flour
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven boiler
  3. Remove skin from peppers
  4. Stuff peppers with cheese
  5. Whisk egg yolks
  6. Serve and Enjoy
